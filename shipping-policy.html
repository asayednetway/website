Shipping Policy

At Netway Corp, we deliver our products and services in a secure and timely manner. Our shipping policy applies to both digital software licenses and physical telecommunications equipment.

1. Delivery of Digital Products

Digital products such as software licenses, activation keys, and downloadable files will be delivered via email or our secure client portal within 24 hours of payment confirmation. In some cases, delivery may take up to 3 business days for verification or customization.

2. Delivery of Physical Products

Physical products, including networking devices, routers, and accessories, are shipped through trusted courier partners within Egypt and internationally.

  • Standard delivery time within Egypt: 3–7 business days.
  • International delivery time: 7–14 business days.
  • Tracking information will be provided once the order is dispatched.

3. Shipping Costs

Shipping charges vary depending on the delivery location, product type, and order weight. The exact cost will be calculated and displayed at checkout.

4. Customs and Import Duties

For international orders, the recipient is responsible for any customs duties, taxes, or import regulations applicable in the destination country.

5. Failed Delivery Attempts

In the event of a failed delivery due to incorrect address details or unavailability, the courier may reattempt delivery or return the item to us. Additional charges may apply for re-shipping.
