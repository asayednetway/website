Refund Policy

At Netway Corp, we are committed to delivering high-quality software solutions, telecommunications services, and AI-based technologies to our valued clients in Egypt and abroad. We understand that circumstances may arise where a refund is necessary, and we strive to ensure that our policy is transparent, fair, and compliant with Egyptian consumer protection laws.

1. Eligibility for Refunds

Refund requests will be considered if submitted within 14 calendar days from the date of purchase or service activation, provided that:

  • The service or software license has not been fully delivered, activated, or consumed.
  • No significant customization, integration, or development work has been completed specifically for the client.
  • The issue prompting the refund request cannot be resolved through technical support or service adjustments.

2. Non-Refundable Services

Certain services are exempt from refunds, including but not limited to:

  • Fully delivered digital products or activated telecom services.
  • Customized software solutions or integrations built to client specifications.
  • Third-party services, licenses, or subscriptions procured on the client’s behalf.
  • Professional consultation fees and training sessions once delivered.

3. Refund Process

To request a refund, clients must contact our customer service department at support@netway-corp.com with the following information:

  • Full name and contact details
  • Order or invoice number
  • Date of purchase or service activation
  • Reason for the refund request, including any supporting documentation or evidence

Once we receive your request, our team will review it and respond within 5 business days. Approved refunds will be processed using the original payment method within 7–14 business days.

4. Legal Compliance

This Refund Policy complies with the Egyptian Consumer Protection Law No. 181 of 2018 and applicable telecommunications regulations. Nothing in this policy affects your statutory rights under Egyptian law.
